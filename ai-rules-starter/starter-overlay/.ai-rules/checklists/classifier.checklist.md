# Classifier Checklist

- Human request captured.
- Constraints copied exactly.
- Acceptance criteria identified.
- Mode recommendation recorded: Lite, Standard, or Strict.
- Scope is small enough to inspect.
- Diff budget is stated.
- Unknowns are listed.
- Stop conditions are explicit.
- Proceed or stop recommendation is recorded.
- Operator response is short and includes status, artifacts, findings, risks,
  and next action.
